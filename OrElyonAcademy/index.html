About Us

OrElyon Academy is a premier tutoring institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth. We offer comprehensive tutoring services for students from K1 through 12th grade, covering a wide range of subjects and specialties.

Our team of highly qualified tutors, all holding Bachelor's degrees from Zayed University in the UAE, are committed to providing personalized learning experiences that cater to each student's unique needs and learning style.

Our Mission

At OrElyon Academy, our mission is to inspire and empower students to reach their full potential. We strive to create a supportive and engaging learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning.

Our goals include:

  • Providing high-quality, personalized tutoring services
  • Helping students build confidence and achieve academic success
  • Offering a comprehensive curriculum that covers all major subjects
  • Preparing students for future academic and professional challenges
